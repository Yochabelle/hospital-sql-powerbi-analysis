 What is the most commonly done surgery, and which gender does it the most?


SELECT gender, ccs_diagnosis_description,ccs_diagnosis_code, MAX(frequency) AS max_frequency
FROM (SELECT gender, ccs_diagnosis_description, ccs_diagnosis_code, COUNT(*) AS frequency
FROM hospital_hip_discharges
GROUP BY ccs_diagnosis_description, ccs_diagnosis_code, gender 
) AS T2
GROUP BY ccs_diagnosis_description, ccs_diagnosis_code, gender
ORDER BY max_frequency DESC
LIMIT 2

Insights: The most frequent surgery is osteoarthritis-related, with women undergoing it more often.
